Hey, it's Panto time again! I really enjoyed last year's panto, so I was delighted to get another shot at it

This time, the panto was "Jack And The Beanstalk". I was to play the part of the Prime Minister. The King's right-hand man. Unfortunately, since the kingdom has been paying huge amounts of money out to keep the Giant happy, the whole government has been laid off. So in fact I am not only the Prime Minister, but the entire Government and Civil Service rolled into one. Guess it must keep me busy!
