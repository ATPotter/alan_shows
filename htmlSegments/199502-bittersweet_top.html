Noel Coward's Bitter Sweet is not a terribly well-known piece, and is not often performed now. In many ways, that is a shame. It is chock-full of beautiful tunes, in a lyrical operetta style.

However, that my be its problem - it is too full of beautiful music, and is longer than today's audiences really want to sit through. And its three acts are also more than they want to stomach.

All in all, I don't think that either the audience or the club really enjoyed this show. But if you ever get a chance to listen to the music, do - it's wonderfully lush, romantic stuff.
