My first year in Stirling introduced me to a different kind of company from those that I had been in before. The company was dominated by the ferocious but absolutely wonderful Anne Ballantyne, yet the music was very disciplined and well performed. The choreographer was organised; the dancers walked around in leotards, the club was simply very professional and highly organised in its outlook. I loved it!

Another interesting aspect was that this club did a two-week run. Starting again on the Monday was tough!

For the first time, I managed to get my wife-to-be to take show photos too - so I even appeared in some of the photos!

Not only did I do the show, but I also got very involved in the club's concert party - I sang in countless church halls and the like throughout Stirling and its environs. I was in my element, I felt instantly that Stirling was a place I wanted to put down roots.

Well, I've not managed it, yet. But there's still time!
