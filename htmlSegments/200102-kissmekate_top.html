It was very strange to repeat a show, and a part so quickly, but I had been thinking about returning to Stirling for some time and this seemed like a good opportunity. And Heather (the choreographer) had been a friend for some time and so I was keen to do Too Darn Hot with her choreography.

So, along I went and felt entirely at home very quickly. We had a great show!

The friend who was taking pictures for me forgot to switch on the flashgun for Too Darn Hot, but we noticed that the pictures in fact turned out rather well. Oh, if only we had kept the darned thing off for future shows!

However, I had one new toy to play with. My first digital camera! It was a Fujifilm Finepix 1400Z, resplendent in its 1280 x 960 1.2 Megapixel resolution! But I had great fun with this camera, and took lots of pictures I really liked. But in truth I don't think I knew just how much of a change to my photography digital was going to make...
