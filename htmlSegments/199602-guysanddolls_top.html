A large number of the people who did the Forth Dimension shows (rehearsing over the summer) were also members of the Bo'ness Amateur Operatic Society (rehearsing over the winter). So, since I had enjoyed working with Forth Dimension, I joined up with Bo'ness too!

One of my great rehearsal memories was from when we were setting "Havana". At one point in this, Sammy - one of the dancers - had to slap my face. One rehearsal, she missed and instead hit my glasses, which went sailing across to the other side of the rehearsal hall. Luckily, they remained intact!
