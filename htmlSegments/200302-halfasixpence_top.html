Wot's this? 'Arf A Sixpence again? (Yes, my accent was indeed as corny as that!)

The good news was that we were in the macrobert again. It was nice to be in a theatre again; the Albert Halls are a very nice concert hall, but that's different from a theatre. The macrobert had been refurbished in the year we were off, and was now the bluest auditorium I had ever seen - blue walls, blue carpet and very blue seats!

And so we did Sixpence. As I've said before, it isn't a favourite show of mine, but we still had a good time doing it.
