I had seen The Boy Friend being performed by Edinburgh Music Theatre a couple of years before, and thought that it would be fun to do. But when I got the part of Bobby, and realised that I was playing opposite Amanda, it gave me pause for thought. She is so good on stage and such a good dancer, I wondered if it would ever be possible to dance beside her and not appear to be out of my depth!

I don't know whether I managed it, but I certainly had a great time trying. The rehearsals and the show itself all went smoothly. And it was lovely to be working with Dorothy again, who had directed me in Carousel way back in... Ulp!... 1991!

As if that wasn't enough to make me feel old, I also got my twenty-five year long service award from NODA, presented to my by Jim Allardyce! It was presented to me on stage after the show - lovely! But then again, I was being given my twenty-five year award in a show where I was playing a seventeen year old. Something wrong there, surely!
