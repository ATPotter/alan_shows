I have no information about this show - no programme, no photos, nothing! So if anyone has any, please get in touch via Stagepics.

One memory I do have of this show is working the "knife board". One character was doing a knife throwing act while another lead was where the knives were being thrown. I had to hide behind the board, pulling a string to release knives which shot through the board, creating the appearance of the knife having been thrown.

One night, the string on the lock snapped, with the lock half-way released. There was nothing I could do but wait as the spring eventually released the knife, long after the knife throwing act was complete!
