I had had such a good time doing Carousel with June Geissler that I wanted to do another show with her as soon as possible, so when I heard the she was choreographing Cabaret I was signed up for it immediately!

One of the highlights was undoubtedly having the cast photo (below - thanks to Alistair Thomas) taken. It was a Sunday lunchtime, in front of the theatre and just beside a very busy main road. I'm amazed we didn't cause an accident!
