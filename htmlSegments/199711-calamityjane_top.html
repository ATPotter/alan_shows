Somehow, I had managed to avoid the "Cowboy Musicals" for many years, but this time they finally caught up with me, as I did Calamity Jane.

On the positive side, I think that this was my first proper stage kiss. On the negative side, one has to say that Danny Gilmartin is one of the most boring people in all of stage musicals!

The show was good fun to do, though from memory the naked lady above the bar of the Golden Garter didn't even survive to the first night!
